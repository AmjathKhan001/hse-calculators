Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to develop a custom calculator?

Custom calculator development typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple calculators can be delivered in 1-2 weeks, while complex multi-parameter calculators may require 4-6 weeks for development and testing.

Do you offer training on how to use the calculators?

Yes! We offer both online and in-person training sessions. Our training covers calculator usage, interpretation of results, and integration into safety management systems. Contact us for training schedules and pricing.

Can I integrate your calculators into my company's intranet?

Absolutely! We offer enterprise solutions with custom branding, integration APIs, and dedicated support. Contact our partnerships team for integration options and pricing.

What safety standards do your calculators comply with?

Our calculators are developed based on OSHA, NIOSH, ANSI, ISO, and other international safety standards. Each calculator clearly indicates which standards it follows in the detailed information section.

Is there a mobile app available?

Our website is fully mobile-responsive and works on all devices. We are currently developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android, expected to launch in Q3 2025.
