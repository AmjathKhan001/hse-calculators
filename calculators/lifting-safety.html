Lifting Safety Calculator

Calculate safe working loads, center of gravity, and lifting equipment requirements for material handling operations.

Lifting Safety Analysis

How to Use This Calculator

1. Load Information: Enter weight and dimensions of the load.

2. Equipment Selection: Select the lifting equipment to be used.

3. Lifting Configuration: Enter lifting angle, number of slings, and safety factor.

4. Calculation: The calculator determines SWL, load distribution, and safety requirements.

Safe Working Load (SWL) Formula
Lift Type SWL Formula Safety Factor Standard
Single Vertical SWL = WLL 5:1 minimum ASME B30.9
Two-Leg Bridle SWL = 2 × WLL × cos(θ/2) 5:1 minimum ASME B30.9
Three-Leg Triangle SWL = 3 × WLL × load factor 5:1 minimum ASME B30.9
Four-Leg Quad SWL = 3 × WLL (assumes 3 legs carry load) 5:1 minimum ASME B30.9
Critical Lift SWL = WLL / 10 10:1 minimum OSHA 1926.1431
